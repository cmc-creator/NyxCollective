2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information you voluntarily provide and information automatically collected through analytics tools.

Information You Provide

  • Contact form submissions — your name, email address, and message when you use the "Start the Conversation" form.
  • Newsletter sign-ups — your email address when you subscribe to our newsletter.
  • Product notify-me requests — your email address when you request early-access notification for a Coming Soon product.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent, referral source, browser type, and general geographic region via Google Analytics 4 (GA4).
  • Cookies — analytics cookies set by Google Analytics, and a localStorage key (nyx_cookie_ok) used to remember your cookie consent preference. No advertising or third-party tracking cookies are used.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your contact form submissions, licensing inquiries, and project requests.
  • To send newsletter updates and product announcements to subscribers (you can unsubscribe at any time).
  • To notify you when a Coming Soon product becomes available (if you signed up).
  • To understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it (via GA4 analytics).

We do not use your information for automated decision-making or profiling.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

We use the following:

  • Google Analytics cookies — these help us understand site traffic. They do not identify you personally. See Google's Privacy Policy for details on how Google handles this data.
  • nyx_cookie_ok (localStorage) — a local key set when you dismiss the cookie consent banner. It is stored only in your browser and never transmitted to any server.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Disabling analytics cookies will not affect your ability to use this site.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may receive data you submit or data about your visit:

  • Formspree (Privacy Policy) — processes contact form, newsletter, and notify-me form submissions. Your email and message are transmitted to Formspree's servers and forwarded to us. Formspree does not sell your data.
  • Google Analytics 4 (Privacy Policy) — collects anonymized usage data. IP addresses are anonymized by default.
  • Vercel (Privacy Policy) — hosts this website. Vercel may log standard web server data (IP address, request path, timestamp) for a limited retention period.

We do not use advertising networks, retargeting pixels, or social media tracking pixels on this site.

6. We Do Not Sell Your Data

NyxCollective LLC does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party, ever. Full stop.

7. Data Retention

Contact form submissions and newsletter sign-ups are retained as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and manage our communications. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us through the contact form.

Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months per our GA4 property settings.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent (e.g., unsubscribe from our newsletter) at any time.
  • Opt out of Google Analytics tracking by using the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

To exercise these rights, contact us through the contact form on the main site.

9. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy
