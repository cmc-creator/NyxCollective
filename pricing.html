Pricing FAQ

Common Questions

Are the prices listed final?

Licensing prices are published as starting points. The exact monthly rate depends on the number of users, integrations required, and any custom configuration. We will confirm the exact number before any agreement is signed.

Is there a contract or minimum term?

No contracts. Licensing is month-to-month. White-label builds are one-time project engagements, not subscriptions. You own the output.

What does "managed hosting" include?

All licensing includes managed hosting, SSL, server maintenance, security patches, and architecture updates. You do not touch infrastructure. We handle everything.

Can I upgrade from a license to a white-label later?

Yes. Many clients start with a license to validate the product in their workflow, then commission a white-label build when ready to own it fully. Contact us to discuss the transition path.

Do you offer non-profit or healthcare discounts?

Yes. We work with healthcare organizations, non-profits, and early-stage startups regularly and can often accommodate adjusted pricing. Reach out and share your context.
