A license gives you direct access to one of our existing products - deployed within our infrastructure and ready to use as-is. White-label is a full custom build where we take the same foundation, rebuild it under your brand identity with any modifications your business needs, and hand it off as yours entirely. Licensing is faster and lower cost. White-label is deeper and fully owned by you.

Most white-label builds are delivered in 2 to 6 weeks depending on the scope of customization. Because we are working from an existing, production-tested foundation rather than building from scratch, timelines are dramatically shorter than a typical custom development engagement. We will give you a firm scope and timeline before any work begins.

Yes. For white-label projects we typically structure payment as a deposit at kickoff and the remainder on delivery. For bundle licensing we can discuss monthly or quarterly arrangements. Reach out through the contact form and we will find a structure that works for your situation.

All licenses include onboarding support and documentation. White-label builds include a post-launch support window and access to our team for questions.
